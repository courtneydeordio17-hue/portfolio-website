Meet the Designer

My Story

Hi, I'm Courtney, based in Denver, Colorado! I’m a UI Designer with a decade of experience in client services across tourism, hospitality, and recreation. Holding a Bachelor of Science in Environmental Education and Interpretation, I’ve always been driven by a deep connection to the exploration and preservation of the natural world.

In my past roles designing bespoke travel itineraries, managing high-value partnerships, and leading operations, I honed the ability to understand client needs and translate them into actionable solutions. Along the way, I mastered skills in relationship building, problem solving, and process optimization, all of which directly inform my design practice today.

Now, I channel that same curiosity, creativity, and user-focused mindset into designing intuitive and engaging interfaces. I’m passionate about developing digital experiences that feel seamless, purposeful, and delightful.

When I’m not immersed in design, you’ll likely find me in the Colorado Rocky Mountains camping, hiking, paddle boarding, skiing, or running. Travel is also central to who I am. Whether exploring U.S. National Parks or experiencing cultures abroad, I thrive on discovery, and I bring that same sense of adventure and perspective to my work.

If you’re looking for a designer who blends creativity with empathy and a problem-solving mindset, I’d love to connect and collaborate.
