My work

Purely Nourished

Purely Nourished is a recipe platform designed for individuals with gluten-free and dairy-free needs, offering a simple, clutter-free alternative to overwhelming recipe apps and blogs. Showcasing a user-centered design approach focused on clarity, accessibility, and responsive functionality.

I applied user flow mapping, wireframing and prototyping to create an intuitive interface that simplifies recipe discovery and preparation. Using responsive grid layouts, clear visual hierarchy, and usability testing, the design ensures a seamless, stress-free experience across all devices.

Switchbacks

Switchbacks is a native mobile app for iOS and Android devices that is designed to help users plan National Park trips.

I conducted competitive app comparisons to identify feature gaps and user expectations, then developed user flows and low-, mid-, and high-fidelity wireframes to map and refine key interactions. Final user testing validated usability and navigation, while attention to visual hierarchy, layout consistency, and interaction design ensured a polished and engaging interface for National Park explorers.
